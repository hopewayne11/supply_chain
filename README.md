# Supply Chain AI Intelligence System

## Overview

The Supply Chain AI Intelligence System is an AI-powered platform designed to help businesses make informed decisions across inventory management, supplier evaluation, logistics, and demand forecasting.

The system combines machine learning, computer vision, and intelligent analytics to transform supply chain data into actionable insights.

---

## Features

### Inventory Management
- Add and manage products
- Monitor stock levels
- Track inventory status

### Vision AI
- Analyze uploaded product images
- Detect damaged or defective goods
- Support product quality assessment

### Demand Forecasting
- Real time prediction with live data (google trends, social media signals, datasets).
- Predict future product demand
- Identify potential stock shortages
- Assist inventory planning

### Supplier Intelligence
- Supplier trust scoring
- Delivery performance analysis
- Fraud and anomaly detection
- Supplier reliability evaluation

### Logistics Tracking
- Shipment monitoring
- Delivery status tracking
- Estimated arrival visibility
